Benjamin Hattemer

I am Benjamin Hattemer, a PhD candidate in economics from the European University Institute. I do applied research in environmental and labour economics. My supervisors are Thomas Crossley and Alexander Monge-Naranjo. .

My main line of research has been related to the physical risk of environmental phenomena on labor market outcomes. Recently, I started working on climate and energy policy issues.
